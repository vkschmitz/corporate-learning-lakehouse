# Databricks Genie AI Space Documentation

## Purpose

The Genie AI Space enables natural language interaction with the Learning Analytics Lakehouse using Databricks Genie concepts.

In this project, the Genie layer prepares educational and behavioral datasets for conversational analytics, semantic exploration and AI-assisted business intelligence.
